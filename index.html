What is Diyora NXT for diamond and jewellery retailers?

Diyora NXT can make shopping for jewellery in your store even better! With our special kiosk and retail management system. you can offer your customers thousands of unique designs from Diyora.

Customers can browse through all the different designs, choose the metal and diamond they like, and place an order. Then, once you approve the order from your dashboard, Diyora will take care of everything else. We'll fulfill the order and deliver it to your store with all the necessary documents.

Diyora NXT features

Realtime Pricing

The Diyora NXT kiosk shows you the current price for the metal and diamond you choose. You can also add some extra cost to it to make a profit when you sell it in your store.

360° Product View

The platform helps customers make decisions faster by showing them a complete view of the product. They can now admire true craftsmanship through a screen!

Customization

Diyora NXT allows you to fully customise everything from your logo on the kiosk to the screensaver and banner inside. You can make it look exactly how you want it to.
